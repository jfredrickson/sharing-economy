Sharing Economy Conference: Boston

This conference will join leaders from business, investment, government, and Universities for a day to discuss the new economic model of sharing in Boston and beyond.

This collaborative approach introduces the idea that ownership and access are somewhat shared between corporations, start-ups, non-governmental organizations, and people. This results in new products, services, and businesses that can combine resources to enable more access, knowledge, tools, and in the end, more productivity.

According to a recent report from Altimeter Group, an independent research firm based in San Mateo, California, the sharing economy is a $2 billion industry already with a project value of over $100 billion.

Topics

  • Advising and Investing in the Sharing Economy

  • How does this fit into a sustainable future?

  • How will the legal scene address the intricacies of sharing?

  • Are any industries not yet involved that would benefit?
